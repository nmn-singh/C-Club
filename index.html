WHO WE ARE

Consulting & Analytics Club, MNNIT ALLAHABAD, widely known as C&A, is a premier student group MNNIT ALLAHABAD.

Connect

The club is a dynamic interface for like-minded individuals operating through ‘knowledge-sessions’. We leverage focus groups and expert interviews to conduct in-depth research on core demand areas to facilitate skill-building among members.

Learn-by-Practice

C&A through its main divisions in Consulting and Data Analytics encourages students to follow a learn-by-practice regimen, and for the same, the club supports and organises a wide range of initiatives and events throughout the year.

Beyond

The club is a dynamic interface for like-minded individuals Our members build on the skills and experience they have gained to join top firms and prestigious institutions across all industries after graduation. Our members have won laurels globally in various competitions. With our initiatives, we support over 25K students and professionals from over 50 nations worldwide annually.
